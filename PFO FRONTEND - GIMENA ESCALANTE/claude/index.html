Our Story

Born from a Love
of the Perfect Cup

Aurora Coffee House opened its doors in 2011 when founder Maya Reyes returned from a year traveling through Ethiopia, Colombia, and Japan — chasing the coffee farms she had read about since childhood. She came back with a duffel bag of green beans and an unshakeable conviction: that truly great coffee deserved a home worthy of it.

What began as a ten-seat café in a converted 1920s shopfront has grown into a neighbourhood institution. We source every bean directly from small-batch farms, roast on-site twice weekly, and train our baristas to treat each extraction as a craft, not a transaction.

Our pastry kitchen runs on the same philosophy: real butter, seasonal fruit, no shortcuts. Everything you eat here was made this morning.

Vegan & Plant-Based
